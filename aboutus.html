About Us

Haukai Restaurant specializes in Māori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands. It was started by a couple who are eager on creating a business that serves authentic Māori and Pākeha kai and drinks to locals and tourists visiting the area.

If you want to learn more about our culture and kai, please download our brochure: The Haukai Restaurant.
